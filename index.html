ABOUT Subteran

On 4th April 2015 the first instalment of Subteran lands in Manchester. Over the course of 2015 Subteran will be hosting a number of nights in Manchester for you to indulge yourself on your love of techno-fused house and underground techno. The night is derived from the passion of its resident Dj's to put a little back into a scene that Manchester lives and breathes. Our nights are designed around the music and you. Nothing more!!

To kick things off we are holding a residents party from the RoadHouse in Manchester's Northern Quarter. Our headliner on the night is none other than Subteran resident and Manchester's very own MATT EVERSON. Matt is no stranger to the Manchester techno scene having graced the decks at the WHP every year since it began in 2007. You'll have seen him at Parklife over the last 3 years and his productions have made it on Radio 1's playlist and signed to Toolroom and Monique Musique. Our remaining residents will be keeping the night flowing with their passion for the very best this music has to offer.

Tickets are Â£5 all night.

This night is about you and the music, nothing more!!

Our doors open at 11pm until 4am. For more info and a taste of what's in store visit our SubteranMcr pages on Twitter/Facebook/Instagram/Resident Advisor and Mixcloud. Join us from the very beginning!!
